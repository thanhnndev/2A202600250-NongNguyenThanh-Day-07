**Micro cut technology – minimally invasive and gentle**

**In the SmartSight procedure**, the SCHWIND ATOS® creates a predefined lenticule in the intrastromal tissue of the cornea, and makes small peripheral incisions in the top corneal layer for lenticular access. This minimally invasive procedure results in fewer nerve transections in the cornea surface, reducing the potential for postoperative incidence of dry eye. 

After lasering, the surgeon, looking through the microscope included in SCHWIND ATOS®, can easily remove the lenticule through the small access incisions. SmartSight uses no corneal flap of any kind, and there is no laser ablation like there is with excimer lasers.
